Pinterest Integration

Daily Deal Finder uses Pinterest as a visual discovery channel for shopping ideas, product inspiration, and helpful buying content.

Our planned Pinterest API usage is limited to managing our own content, creating pins for our own published pages, and organizing boards related to our shopping categories.

We do not use Pinterest to send spam, scrape private user data, or publish misleading content.

Any affiliate relationship is disclosed on our website, and our Pinterest activity is intended to help users discover content that is already available on Daily Deal Finder.
