1. Introduction

Welcome to Daily Deal Finder. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you visit our website, interact with our content, or contact us.

2. Who We Are

Daily Deal Finder is an informational website dedicated to helping visitors discover carefully selected products, shopping inspiration, buying guides, and useful online resources.

Website: https://daily-deal-finder.com

3. Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address when you contact us. We may also collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referring website, and cookies or similar technologies.

4. Pinterest API Integration

When you choose to connect your Pinterest Business account to our application through Pinterest OAuth, we only access the information and permissions that you explicitly authorize.

Depending on the permissions you grant, this may include your public profile, boards, Pins, and publishing actions required to provide our services.

We never publish, edit, delete, or modify Pinterest content without your explicit action and authorization.

We do not access private messages, passwords, payment information, or other sensitive personal data. We do not sell, rent, or share Pinterest user data with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

4a. OAuth Authorization Process

Daily Deal Finder uses Pinterest OAuth 2.0 to request access to your Pinterest account. During the authorization process, you will be redirected to Pinterest's official website where you can review and approve the specific permissions we request.

We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our service: reading your public profile and boards, and creating Pins on your behalf when you explicitly initiate that action.

We do not and cannot access your Pinterest password at any point in this process.

4b. Token Storage and Data Retention

When you authorize our application, Pinterest provides us with an access token and a refresh token. These tokens are stored securely using industry-standard encryption and are used solely to maintain your authorized connection to our service.

Access tokens have a limited lifespan. Refresh tokens are used to obtain new access tokens without requiring you to re-authorize. We do not use these tokens for any purpose other than providing the specific functionality you have approved.

You can revoke access at any time through your Pinterest account settings. Upon revocation, all stored Pinterest tokens and associated session data are permanently deleted from our systems.
