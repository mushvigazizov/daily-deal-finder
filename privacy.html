Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Daily Deal Finder does not require user registration and does not collect personal information through forms on this website. We do not store, collect, or process personal data unless voluntarily provided through email contact.

When you contact us via email, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is never shared, sold, or used for marketing purposes.

2. Cookies and Tracking

Daily Deal Finder does not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any cookie-based tracking technology. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar third-party tracking services.

3. Affiliate Links

Some links on this website may be affiliate links, including Amazon Associates links. When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, the retailer may place a cookie in your browser to track that the referral came from Daily Deal Finder. We do not have access to or control over these third-party cookies.

4. Pinterest API Integration

When you choose to connect your Pinterest Business account through Pinterest OAuth, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant. We request only the minimum permissions necessary: reading your public profile and boards, and creating Pins on your behalf when you explicitly initiate that action.

We do not and cannot access your Pinterest password. We do not publish, edit, delete, or modify Pinterest content without your explicit action and authorization. We do not access private messages, passwords, payment information, or other sensitive personal data.

5. Token Storage and Data Retention

When you authorize our application, Pinterest provides us with an access token and a refresh token. These tokens are stored securely and used solely to maintain your authorized connection. You can revoke access at any time through your Pinterest account settings. Upon revocation, all stored Pinterest tokens are permanently deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (Pinterest API, Amazon Associates, OpenAI API) to provide our service. These services have their own privacy policies that govern their use of data.

7. Your Rights (GDPR/EEA)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You may also object to processing and have the right to data portability. Contact us at mushvig.azizov@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: mushvig.azizov@gmail.com
